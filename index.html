About

Jim Pappalardo, MBA — financial clarity for Main Street.

Jim Pappalardo brings corporate finance discipline to small and mid-size businesses throughout Central Florida. His background spans financial planning and analysis, budgeting, long-range forecasting, and strategic advisory — experience built inside companies before being made available to owners who need that same level of rigor without a full finance department.

Jim holds a BS in Economics and an MBA from Stetson University, and serves on DeBary's City Council. Jim will complete his CPA license later in 2026 as well as complete his Masters of Accountancy in December 2026.

His early career included financial analysis and budgeting roles in the telecom and healthcare sectors, where he built annual budgets, long-term five-year forecasts, and capital and staffing plans. That foundation in corporate finance — the kind that supports real decisions — is what drives Pappalardo Consulting today.
