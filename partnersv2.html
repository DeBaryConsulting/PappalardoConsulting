Partner options

Choose the model that fits your firm.

Direct referral

You introduce, we contract, you get rewarded.

Best for firms that want to keep the relationship simple. You send the lead, we manage the engagement, and you earn a revenue-based referral fee when the client signs and pays.

White-label

We operate behind your brand.

Best for firms that want to offer premium finance support without building the capability in-house. You own the client relationship, and we deliver under your name as a subcontractor.
